iSMC
----
Mac Unseen bundles the arm64 slice of the official iSMC v0.16.5
command-line binary. The packaging process removes unused architectures
and debugging symbols but does not change program behavior.

Copyright (c) Daniel Korunic and contributors
License: GNU General Public License v3.0
Source: https://github.com/dkorunic/iSMC
Revision: 9b21ebd2d2a5e8e396e64e7142570c955638a600

The iSMC v0.16.5 project source is included under Resources/Source.
See OPEN_SOURCE_COMPLIANCE.md for the additional corresponding-source
condition that applies before commercial redistribution.
Mac Unseen also builds a small read-only fan-speed probe from iSMC's GPL
AppleSMC transport. Its source, transport source, license, and standalone
build instructions are included under Resources/Source/FanSpeedProbe.

iSMC's statically linked runtime dependency notices are included under
Resources/Licenses/iSMC-Dependencies.

Private API notice
------------------
Apple does not document the SPU and MultitouchSupport interfaces used here.
They may change in future macOS releases. Mac Unseen only reads sensor
reports and does not change fan curves, power limits, or hardware settings.
